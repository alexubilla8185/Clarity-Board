Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Clarity Board. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application. By using Clarity Board, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data Storage

Clarity Board is a privacy-first application. All data you create, including projects, notes, checklists, categories, and settings, is stored exclusively on your local device within your web browser's storage (IndexedDB). Your data is never sent to, or stored on, any external servers controlled by us.

3. AI Features

Our application offers optional AI features powered by the Google Gemini API. When you choose to use an AI feature (such as "Smart Split" or "Enhance Text"), only the specific text you provide for that feature is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. We do not send any other personal data or project information. Your use of these features is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

4. Data Export and Import

You have the option to export all your application data as a local JSON file for backup purposes. Similarly, you can import a previously exported backup file to restore your data. This process is entirely managed by you on your local device.

5. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics services, or other mechanisms to monitor your usage of the application. We do not collect any personal information, IP addresses, or device information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
